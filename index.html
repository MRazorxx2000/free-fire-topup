Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our top-up service.

How long does the top-up take?

Most top-ups are completed within 5-15 minutes after payment confirmation. In rare cases, it may take up to 30 minutes depending on server load. You will receive a notification once your diamonds have been added to your account.

Is my account information safe?

Absolutely! We only require your Free Fire UID and nickname. We never ask for your password or any sensitive account information. All data is encrypted and stored securely.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Dialog eZ Cash, Bank Transfer, Visa, Mastercard, and PayPal. For Sri Lankan customers, we recommend Dialog eZ Cash for the fastest processing.

What if my top-up fails?

In the rare event of a failed top-up, we offer a 100% money-back guarantee. Contact our support team within 24 hours and we will either retry the top-up or process a full refund.

Can I top-up for a friend?

Yes! Simply enter your friend's Free Fire UID and nickname during checkout. The diamonds will be credited to their account. Make sure to double-check the UID before confirming.
